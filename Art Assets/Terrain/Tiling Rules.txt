Tile Rules (Below is a list of all tiles, with which tiles they can connect too assuming they are the one being placed.)
Tiles may be connected to different tiles so long as they are all on the list for the tile that is itself being placed.
Tiles that can only connect to a handfull of tile types should be by far the rarest.
All tiles can be rotated and mirrored at random unless otherwise noted.
